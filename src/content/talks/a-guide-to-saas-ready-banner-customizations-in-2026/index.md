Customization isn’t dead — it’s evolved. This strategy session shows developers, architects, and IT leaders how to make Ellucian Banner customizations SaaS-safe and governable. Learn the target architecture with Ellucian Ethos, operating model, guardrails, and decision frameworks — plus real examples from the University of Manitoba to replace DB tweaks with governed extensions and avoid shadow IT.
