Mixtape is a game full of feelings and nostalgia for GenX-ers and aged Aged Millennials like myself from Beethoven & Dinosaur. It's a fun narrative experience that I enjoyed, but won't be going back to anytime soon.

## What I Didn't Like

### Linearity and Nothing Really Matters

Mixtape keeps you on a linear timeline, which makes perfect sense for the game. It goes through the soundtrack, as known as a mixtape, and each song represents a chapter in story being told by our protagonist.

Even though it made sense, I would have liked some choice. Some option to see what else was in this world.

But it wasn't there, and that's okay-- but now that I'm done with it, I won't be going back.

## What I Liked

### Art and Sound

The art and sound are perfect.

They reminded me of the pretencious music and art nerds I knew in university, and this game just nails it.

The soundtrack a wide variety of songs, from the 60's to the 90's. Most of them I didn't recognize, however it didn't master. It was like being at a party with a bunch of music nerds that had to play this one song from that one band that did the thing once in a garage in 1977 and so on and so on.

I have no doubt there is are music trivia nerds that will be all over this stuff. I'm not that person, but I can certainly enjoy the ride and the vibes.

### Short, but Sweet

This game took me about three hours to beat, start to finish. That was the right duration.

Play might be a strong word for this one, but it's very much a game that plays like a movie. Movies shouldn't be more than 3 hours long. In fact, if I were better at games and didn't have to explore every nook and cranny, I probably would been done in two hours, and it would have been just as good.

## The Conclusion

If Ferris Buhler's Day Off was a video game, it would be Mixtape.

I can see how people think Mixtape is a "non-game" or "not a real game", but it's a game. It uses the medium of video game to get a story across, but the player is really just along for the ride and doesn't really feel necessary.

All in all, I enjoyed myself and recommend others to give it a shot if they want to kill a few hours. It's a great game to watch. It's fun game to play. What else could you ask for?
